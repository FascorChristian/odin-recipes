Pastel de Choclo

"Pastel de choclo is a traditional Chilean dish with a sweet corn topping, layered over a savory filling of beef, chicken, onions, and olives, baked until golden."

Ingredients

  • Fresh corn kernels — 6–8 cups
  • Ground beef — 400–500 g
  • Shredded chicken breast (optional) — 200 g
  • Chopped onions — 1–2 units
  • Hard-boiled eggs — 3–4 units
  • Black olives — 8–12 units
  • Fresh basil (to taste)
  • Butter — 2–3 tablespoons
  • Milk or cream — 100–200 ml (for the corn mixture)
  • Sugar — 1–2 tablespoons (to sprinkle on top)
  • Salt, pepper, and cumin (to taste)
  • Cooking oil

Cooking steps

  1. Cook the ground beef with onions and seasonings.
  2. Shred the chicken and set aside.
  3. Blend the corn with milk or cream, then cook with butter and basil until thick.
  4. Place the meat, chicken, olives, and hard-boiled eggs in a baking dish.
  5. Cover with the corn mixture.
  6. Sprinkle sugar on top.
  7. Bake until golden and slightly caramelized.
