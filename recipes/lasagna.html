Lasagna

"Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy b√©chamel, and melted cheese baked to perfection."

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets of pasta
  • ground beef
  • tomato paste
  • bechamel cream
  • cheese
  • garlic and other species

Cooking steps

  1. Cook the ground beef with a little oil or unsalted butter in a pan
  2. Add garlic and aromatics when the beef its halfway through
  3. At medium heat combine the beef with tomato paste. Mix and stir until the mixture starts bubbling, then go on low heat
  4. in a pot with boiling water add the pasta sheets. Prepare a bowl with cold water and ice.
  5. When the pasta it al dente take it out and put it in the ice water for a minute or to, then on a dry plate
  6. add the becheml to the pan and cheese and preheat the oven
  7. when the mixture is done starts assembly the lasagna in an oven tray
  8. wrap the top of the tray with tin foil and put it inside the oven at high heat for 30 minutes
  9. take it out and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving
