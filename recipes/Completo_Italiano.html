Completo Italiano

"The Chilean completo italiano is a hot dog topped with fresh tomato, mashed avocado, and mayonnaise, resembling the colors of the Italian flag."

Ingredients

  • Hot dog bun
  • Sausage (frankfurter)
  • Tomato (diced)
  • Mashed avocado
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salt (to taste)

Cooking steps

  1. Cook the sausage and place it in the bun.
  2. Top with diced tomato.
  3. Add mashed avocado.
  4. Finish with mayonnaise.
